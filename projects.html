Vision:

Computer vision applications are becoming much more pervasive in society. Cameras are everywhere, so it is advantageous to create new methods for interacting and learning from these devices. I'm interested in developing tools for real-time vision capable of detecting physical structure and for object recognition.

Haptics:

Adding the sense of touch to simulations and remote devices can provide a much more immersive interface for human-machine interaction. I'm interested in investigating haptic guidance particularly for medical applications.

Robotics:

I am interested in developing methods for perception that enable robots to work with humans to perform tasks such as assisted surgery. I think that robots should help - not necessarily replace - humans for dangerous and precise activities.
